Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers on orders, delivery, returns, and custom requests.

1. Do you deliver across Kenya?

Yes. We deliver around Kenya including Nairobi, major towns, and remote areas through courier partners.

2. How long does delivery take?

Nairobi is usually same day or next day. Major towns are typically 1 to 2 business days. Remote areas may take 2 to 4 business days.

3. Can I order custom products?

Yes. We support custom printing, modified electronics, personalized beauty items, home products, and sports gear.

4. What if I need an item you do not have?

You can still request it. If we do not have it, you get 10% off your next purchase.

5. Which payment methods do you accept?

We support common Kenya-friendly payment options including M-Pesa and other agreed methods during checkout confirmation.

6. What is your returns policy?

Returns are accepted within 7 days for wrong, damaged, or defective items. Custom/personalized items are returnable only if defective.

7. How do I contact support quickly?

Use WhatsApp for fastest support: +254 118 174145.
