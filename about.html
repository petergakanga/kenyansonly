About KENYANSONLY

Quality Products for Every Kenyan.

Our Story

KENYANSONLY was created as a nostalgia marketplace where childhood experiences are turned into creative memory products.

The reason I made this website is because I am jobless and idle, so I decided to use my time to build something meaningful and practical online.

Our collection is inspired by real moments many of us remember: ants that disciplined us, cookies that fell, school punishments, playground drama, and all the little stories that shaped us.

Today, the website focuses on sharing those memories in a fun way, with simple shopping, prices in Kenyan shillings, and a local story-driven identity.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To preserve shared childhood experiences by turning them into creative memory products that feel local, relatable, and fun.

Our Vision

To build the most loved Kenyan nostalgia brand where everyday memories become stories people can collect and share.

Our Values

Authenticity, humor, local identity, fair KES pricing, reliable service, and continuous improvement.

Why Choose KENYANSONLY?

  • Relatable Products - Every item is inspired by real childhood moments people actually remember
  • Kenyan Identity - The stories, tone, and references are rooted in everyday local experience
  • Creative Packaging - Memories are presented as collectible products with clear themes
  • KES Pricing - Transparent prices in Kenyan shillings with no currency confusion
  • Simple Shopping - Easy browsing, filtering, and checkout flow across desktop and mobile
  • WhatsApp Support - Fast support for questions, orders, and custom requests
  • Fresh Updates - New memory ideas and collections are added regularly
  • Story-Driven Brand - More than a store, it is a fun archive of the moments that raised us
