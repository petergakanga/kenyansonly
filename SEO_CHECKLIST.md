## üìù Content Tips for Better SEO

### Product Titles
‚ùå Bad: "Insects"
‚úÖ Good: "Red Ant Colony with Queen (500-1000 workers) - Premium Brazilian Specimen"

### Product Descriptions
- Include: What it is, its benefits, specifications, suitable for whom
- Use keywords naturally (2-3 per description)
- Include measurements and quantities
- Add care instructions where relevant

### Meta Descriptions
- 155-160 characters
- Include main keyword
- Include unique selling point
- Call to action

### Headers (H1, H2, H3)
- Only ONE H1 per page (your main title)
- Use H2 for major sections
- Use H3 for subsections
- Include relevant keywords naturally
