Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver across Kenya, including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, Thika, and surrounding towns.

For remote areas, we still deliver using the best available courier route.

Delivery Timelines

Location Estimated Delivery Time Starting Fee
Nairobi Same day or next day KES 300
Major towns 1 to 2 business days KES 450
Remote areas 2 to 4 business days KES 600+

How Delivery Works

  • Place your order on the website or WhatsApp.
  • We confirm stock, location, and final delivery fee.
  • Order is prepared and dispatched.
  • You receive progress updates until delivery is completed.

Important Notes

  • Orders confirmed before 3:00 PM are processed the same day.
  • Public holidays and severe weather may affect timelines.
  • For urgent delivery requests, contact us first on WhatsApp: +254 118 174145.
