Returns Policy

Simple and fair returns for Kenya deliveries.

Return Window

You can request a return within 7 days of receiving your order.

Eligible Returns

  • Wrong item delivered.
  • Item delivered damaged or defective.
  • Item in original condition with packaging and proof of purchase.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom printed or personalized products, unless there is a defect or production error.
  • Modified products once accepted and used, unless they arrive defective.
  • Items damaged after delivery due to misuse.

How To Request a Return

  • Send your order number and photos via WhatsApp: +254 118 174145.
  • Our support team verifies your request.
  • If approved, we guide pickup or return delivery steps.
  • Replacement or refund is processed after inspection.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 3 to 7 business days using your original payment method or agreed alternative.
