## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "I don't see my site in Google"
- **Wait**: Indexing takes 1-4 weeks
- **Do**: Submit sitemap to Search Console
- **Check**: robots.txt isn't blocking crawlers

### "No organic traffic yet"
- **Normal**: Takes 2-3 months minimum
- **Check**: Keywords are realistic
- **Improve**: Content quality and quantity

### "Rankings dropped"
- **Check**: Did you change content significantly?
- **Check**: Are there technical errors?
- **Monitor**: Google Search Console for issues

### "Site slower than expected"
- **Optimize**: Image sizes (compress)
- **Check**: .htaccess caching is configured
- **Use**: CDN for static files (optional)
